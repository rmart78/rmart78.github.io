About Rock Salt

Started in 2015, Rock Salt attempts to find meaning in the sometimes trivial, and other times profound, things that make up the world around us.

The things I write about will often be random, but I promise to never knee-jerk; if I've put it here, that means I've put some thought into it - whether you agree with it or not.
